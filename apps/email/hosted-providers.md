# Hosted email providers

## Migadu
- https://www.migadu.com/
- No calendar, just email with a contactbook
- Custom webmail
- Swiss-based
- Micro tier:
  - price: $19/year
  - storage: 5GB
  - emails/day: 200 in, 20 out


## Mailcheap
- https://www.mailcheap.co/email-shared.html
- Has calendar
- Sogo & Afterlogic
- USA based
- No "delete account"
- Cheapest option:
  - price: $24/year
  - storage: 10GB
